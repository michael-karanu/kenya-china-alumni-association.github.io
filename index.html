About us

Our Story

The Kenya China Alumni Assocition has been in existence for 20 years and was formed to connect students who studied in China and enhance cooperation between China and Kenya.

We have a variety of professional groups and training workshops for alumni in different fields including business, tourism, marketing and education that help one become the best they can become in their field. Our wide network ensures you can access mentors in any domain.

We have an online library and news page that keep members updated on the latest developments in the chinese language and Sino-African relations. We also have business, teachers and scolarship groups that help members access opportunites for further for further education and get chinese teaching jobs

We believe our knowledge of the Chinese language and culture is useful in promoting harmony between Kenya and Chinese speakers. We regularly have visits at schools and other institutions to encourage learning Chinese and donate learning materials.
